【任务目标】

对输入的地图瓦片进行视觉风格化处理，但必须严格保持地图作为真实地理信息载体的准确性。输出结果应当在视觉风格上发生变化，但在地图结构、坐标对应、道路网络、水系、建筑、边界、文字、POI 和瓦片边缘衔接关系上保持与原始输入完全一致。

The task is to visually stylize the input map tile while strictly preserving its function as an accurate geographic map. The output may change in visual style, color, texture, and artistic rendering, but the map geometry, coordinate correspondence, road network, waterways, buildings, boundaries, labels, POIs, and tile-edge continuity must remain identical to the source.
